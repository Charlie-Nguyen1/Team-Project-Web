## ğŸ¨ Design System Overview

### Design Philosophy
The design follows modern Material Design principles with a focus on:
- **Professional Appearance**: Enterprise-grade dental practice management
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Intuitive navigation for dental staff
- **Security-First**: Protection of sensitive patient data (HIPAA compliant approach)
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Accessibility**: WCAG compliance with proper contrast and semantic HTML

### Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: React 19, Material-UI v7, Vite
- **Backend**: Express.js, Node.js
- **Database**: MongoDB Atlas (Cloud)
- **Authentication**: JWT (JSON Web Tokens)
- **Hosting**: Render.com
